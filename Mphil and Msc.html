Mphil and Msc

The University of Ghana is a prestigious public university located in Accra, Ghana. The Department of Computer Science (DCS) at UG offers two postgraduate degree programs: Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science and Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science.

Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science

The MSc in Computer Science is a one-year program designed for graduates who wish to acquire advanced knowledge and skills in computer science. The program covers a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, operating systems, computer architecture, computer networks, artificial intelligence, computer graphics, computer security, database systems, software engineering, and web development.

Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science

The MPhil in Computer Science is a two-year program designed for graduates who wish to pursue a research degree in computer science. The program emphasizes original research and requires students to complete a thesis. The program covers a wide range of research areas, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, operating systems, computer architecture, computer networks, artificial intelligence, computer graphics, computer security, database systems, software engineering, and web development.
