Undergraduate

The University of Ghana (UG) is the premier university in Ghana and one of the most prestigious universities in Africa. The Department of Computer Science (DCS) at UG offers two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major and Major-Minor as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major

This is a four-year program that provides students with a strong foundation in the core areas of computer science, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, operating systems, computer architecture, and computer networks. Students also have the opportunity to specialize in one of the following areas: artificial intelligence, computer graphics, computer security, database systems, software engineering, and web development

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Major-Minor

This is a four-year program that allows students to major in computer science and minor in another subject, such as Mathematics and Statistics

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

This is a four-year program that provides students with a broad understanding of information technology, including computer hardware, software, and networking. Students also have the opportunity to specialize in one of the following areas: computer systems, web development, and mobile application development.
